To use the tool, first upload a valid image on the "Choose File" option.
If you are unable to upload the image, most likely the image is too large or is not a supported file format.
To fix this, you can try uploading your pixel art to the myriad of online pixel-art tools to convert it to a proper pixel art image and try it again here!

The image is uploaded successfully if you can see your image beside the label “Input Image” you have!
Then you can now select the settings you want then click the “Process” button!

This will generate the color mapped image of your pixel art with the colors available in Core Keeper.
You can now also use the “Draw Grid Preview” button to show a more detailed preview of each grid!
Remember the X and Y coordinates start from the top-left and moves down and right as you increase X and Y.
